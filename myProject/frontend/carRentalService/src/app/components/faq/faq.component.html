Car Rental FAQ

General Questions

  • Q: How can I rent a car?

    A: To rent a car, you can visit our website and follow the easy steps on our booking page.

  • Q: What are the requirements to rent a car?

    A: You need to be at least 21 years old, have a valid driver's license, and a valid credit card.
